Subjects Manager

The Subjects Manager allows you to organise your classes, topics, and study areas. Each subject you create can be linked to your timetable and displayed on the dashboard. All subjects are stored locally using localStorage.

Creating a Subject

To create a new subject, open the Subjects page and click the Add Subject button. You can enter:

  • Subject name ‚Äî e.g., Mathematics, English, Biology
  • Colour tag ‚Äî used in the timetable and dashboard
  • Description ‚Äî optional notes about the subject

Editing a Subject

Clicking any subject opens its edit panel. You can update the name, colour, or description. Changes are saved instantly.

Deleting a Subject

When you delete a subject:

  • it is removed from the Subjects list
  • its entry is removed from localStorage
  • any timetable entries using that subject will show as ‚ÄúUnassigned‚Äù

How Subjects Connect to the Timetable

When adding a class to your Monday‚ÄìFriday timetable, you select a subject from your list. This allows StudyHub to:

  • display subject colours in the timetable grid
  • show upcoming subjects on the dashboard
  • organise your study schedule visually

Limitations

Because subjects are stored locally:

  • they do not sync across devices
  • clearing browser data removes them
  • timetable entries referencing deleted subjects will lose their labels
